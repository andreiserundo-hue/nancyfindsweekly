Editable + auto-laddering scorecard benchmarks

- Scorecard targets (Good/Better/Best) are now editable inline ("Edit
  targets" -> Save / Cancel / Reset to defaults), persisted to benchmarks.json
- Auto-upgrade: when a metric's L7D value hits Best, the row highlights and
  shows a "hit - upgrade" badge that ladders the whole scale up (down-metrics
  tighten); not reached -> benchmark stays put
- Direction-aware tagging (up vs down metrics) for dots and target display
- server.js: GET/PUT /api/benchmarks persisted to benchmarks.json
